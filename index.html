About Us

Founded in 2024, DANNA BEVERAGES is dedicated to providing pure mineral water sourced from natural water sources, filtered through sand filters, activated carbon medium, micron filters, UV treatment, and ozonated to inhibit the growth of any biofilm. Our water is naturally filtered and rich in essential minerals, promoting health and wellness We prioritize quality, with rigorous testing and certifications ensuring the highest standards.

Our commitment to sustainability drives us to implement eco-friendly practices throughout our production process.

At DANNA BEVERAGES, we believe in delivering not just water, but a refreshing experience

that supports our customers and community.
